    Dancing With The Stars professional dancer, Brandon Armstrong, and his wife, Brylee Ivers, recently shared a heartwarming ballroom dance audition on social media. The couple, who met online in early 2021 and tied the knot in July of the following year, showcased their impressive dance moves to the delight of their fans.

    Brandon Armstrong Dances With His Wife

    Recently, Armstrong and Ivers shared a glimpse of their love and shared passion for dance by posting a delightful ballroom dance audition video. Dancing to the rhythm of “Virgen” by Adolescent’s Orquesta. The couple gracefully swayed and twirled in perfect harmony.

    Despite it being Ivers’ first time attempting ballroom dancing, she showcased natural talent and grace, under the guidance of her experienced husband. In the caption of the Instagram reel, Armstrong playfully wrote that the routine is Ivers’ audition to DWTS. Although he was probably just joking, it still caught the attention of Armstrong’s fans, as well as fellow DWTS pros.

    Fellow pro Jenna Johnson couldn’t contain her enthusiasm and expressed interest in collaborating with Armstrong and Ivers. Armstrong playfully replied that a combination with Johnson and Chmerkovskiy might take a bit longer than a week.

    Britt Stewart also chimed in, showing her love for the adorable performance. The encouragement continued with Daniella Karagach, Alan Bersten, and other pros, showering Ivers with praises and applause for her impressive dance debut.

    The Couple Married In 2022

    Armstrong and Ivers’ love story began in early 2021 when they connected through an app similar to Tinder, specifically for their Church community. After a series of uninspiring dates, Armstrong spotted Ivers’ profile and took a chance by reaching out to her on Instagram.

    After finally getting in touch, Armstrong and Ivers spent a month talking and getting to know each other. Their conversations deepened their connection, and they soon went on their first date. As their bond grew stronger, Brylee took to Instagram on Valentine’s Day to express her admiration for Armstrong, emphasizing his unconditional love, positive outlook on life, and the happiness she felt in his presence.

    In March 2022, Armstrong took a decisive step in their relationship by proposing to Ivers. With a heartfelt Instagram post, he declared his love for her and revealed that asking her to be his wife was the best decision of his life. The couple exchanged vows a few months later in a touching ceremony held in Utah.
