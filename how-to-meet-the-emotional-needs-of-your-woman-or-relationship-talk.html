    Though the emotional needs of women may vary, it is your duty to find out her needs and meet them.

    Do go into a relationship, expecting her to validate you, or make you feel worthwhile and happy. You have got it all wrong.

    Here are some common emotional needs of women.

    1. Honesty: Women love men who are open and sincere. It gives them a sense of emotional security when they can tell what their man is up to and who he is exactly. They trust you more, when you are predictable.

    ADVERTISEMENT

    2. Communication: Women are expressive, and they expect to receive feedback. Communicating with your partner is a sure way to build rapport and get to know each other well. It is important while communicating, that you pay attention to her, no matter how “funny” it sounds. It makes them happy, when their man takes out time to listen to them.

    3. Affection: Women want to be appreciated. They want to know they are loved and the only way to let them know is by acting affectionately. Showing them care and concern. They want to hear words like: “You mean so much to me,” “I will always cherish the love we share.” Etc.

    4. Finances: Money is the root cause of many fights in relationships. The availability or lack of it, all the same. Find out her financial needs and spending habit. It is best to draw out a family budget. Also plan to save.

    5. Commitment: Commitment might mean various things to different women. It could be helping with the chores, for some other, it could be taking care of the children , while it could mean spending time with the family and teaching the kids. Understand her needs and fit in.
