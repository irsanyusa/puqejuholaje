    Singer gets bloody in graphic B*tch better have my money video

    With graphic scenes reminiscent of a Quentin Tarantino film or slash horror and chainsaws that would make serial killers jealous, the video directed by Rihanna and Megaforcewill no doubt spark a flood of controversy.

    09 Sep 2024, 00:00

    With graphic scenes reminiscent of a Quentin Tarantino film or slash horror and chainsaws that would make serial killers jealous, the video directed by Rihanna and Megaforce will no doubt spark a flood of controversy.

    After a civilized opening featuring a wealthy but doomed woman, the action quickly took a very dark turn when the singer exited a hotel room with a huge case and a fluffy Pomeranian in tow.

    A naked blonde is soon seen swinging upside down in a warehouse as Rihanna casually looks on with a cigarette keeping her busy.

    “Don’t act like you forgot, I pull the shots,” she sings menacingly. “Pay me what you owe, don’t act like you forgot.”

    Shots of desolate deserts, luxury yachts and burning cars follow as Rihanna and her partners in crime — or in revenge — reap chaos to get what is owed them.

    Actor Eric Roberts makes an appearance playing a police sheriff leering over the girls as they frolic not-so-innocently in a pool as a dead body floats below the surface.

    Meanwhile “Hannibal” star Mads Mikkelsen, playing “The Accountant,” is not so fortunate and ends up taped to a chair as his nemesis approaches with a sharp knife.

    The hashtag #BBHMMVideo was quickly a top Twitter trend and Tidal tweeted that it was showing the video without a subscription.
