    America’s Got Talent is giving away tickets to the Superstars Live show in Las Vegas. The contest is running into September of this year, so fans have plenty of time to enter the sweepstakes for their chance to win.

    America’s Got Talent Holds Sweepstakes for Las Vegas Tickets

    On Wednesday, AGT tweeted information about the new sweepstakes. Fans can enter for a chance to win a trip for four to see the AGT Superstars Live show in Las Vegas at Luxor Hotel & Casino. The show features past contestants showing off their talents.

    “Come back EVERY WEEK between now and September 27 to increase your chances of winning!” the tweet reads, with a link to where fans can enter the sweepstakes.

    The fine print specifies that you must be at least 21 years old to enter the sweepstakes. You can also read the official rules for more information about whether or not you’re eligible. In order to enter, you’ll have to create an NBCUniversal profile.

    According to the rules, fans can enter once per week, for a total of 15 entries per person before the sweepstakes closes on September 27. The prize includes roundtrip air transportation for the winner and guests, two nights at Luxor Hotel & Casino, $500 spending money, and four tickets to the show.

    Who Is Performing in the Las Vegas Show?

    Recently, the Superstars Live show in Las Vegas has featured AGT Season 14 winner Kodi Lee, Season 17 magician Mervant Vera, and Season 17 comedian Mike E. Winfield. AGT: All-Stars champion Aidan Bryant has also been performing in the show.

    The show is described as a “75-minute fast-paced, high action variety show” which “features show-stopping moments ranging from thrill acts and magicians to vocalists and acrobats.” Fans can also purchase VIP packages, which offer photo opportunities and meet and greets.
