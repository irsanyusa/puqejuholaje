    Harry Styles is rumored to have a new romantic interest in Australian fashion blogger and influencer Yan Yan Chan. According to Entertainment Tonight, the pair recently spent time together during Styles’ Love On Tour shows in Australia.

    Is Harry Styles Dating Yan Yan Chan?

    The news comes months after it was reported that Styles and Olivia Wilde had called it quits after almost two years of dating. It’s not clear how long they have known each other, but a source revealed that they have apparently been acquaintances for some time.

    Styles reportedly invited Chan to a private after-party in Sydney following his concert on March 4. He is said to enjoy spending time with good company while on tour. The source added that Chan is “by all accounts a lot of fun.” The two were also spotted together in Byron Bay during Styles’ secret getaway to Australia in February.

    This news comes months after Styles and actress Olivia Wilde reportedly called it quits after nearly two years of dating. A source told PEOPLE in November that the split was amicable, and that the two remained close friends. Wilde is said to be focusing on her work and children in Los Angeles. While Styles continues to tour and work on new music.

    It remains to be seen whether Styles and Chan’s rumored relationship will develop into something more serious, or if it will remain just a fun fling. In any case, fans of the singer will be watching closely for any official announcements or updates.

    What We Know About Styles’ Rumored Girlfriend Yan Yan Chan

    With rumors of a possible romance between Styles and Chan, fans are eager for details about the Australian influencer. Some suspect she may have captured the singer’s heart.

    According to reports, Chan has been active in the fashion and lifestyle scene in Australia for several years. She has built up a large following on social media. She has also worked as a model, and is known for her chic sense of style and her ability to stay on top of the latest trends.

    Despite her rising profile, Chan has managed to keep much of her personal life out of the public eye. She has not commented publicly on her rumored relationship with Styles. She has instead focused on sharing content related to fashion, beauty, and travel on her social media platforms.

    Based on what little information is available about Chan, it seems that she and Styles share a mutual love of fashion and style. With Styles‘ high-profile status as one of the biggest pop stars in the world, any new romantic interest in his life is sure to generate plenty of attention and speculation.
