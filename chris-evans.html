    31 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Profession: Actor

    Biography: Chris Evans is most famous for his superhero roles within the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

    Evans has played Captain America in stand alone films and in Avengers ensemble films opposite Robert Downey Jr., Chris Hemsworth, Scarlett Johansson and Mark Ruffalo.

    Evans has also portrayed Human Torch in two Fantastic Four films.

    Born: June 13, 1981
    Birthplace: Boston, Massachusetts, USA
    Age: 43 years old

    Generation: Millennial
    Chinese Zodiac: Rooster
    Star Sign: Gemini
