    Brian Justin Crum is a force to be reckoned with on stage, and he’s ready to prove it once again on America’s Got Talent: Fantasy League. The powerhouse vocalist is returning to the show for a second chance at the title, and he’s bringing his incredible voice and captivating stage presence back to the stage.

    Who is Brian Justin Crum?

    Born and raised in San Diego, California, Brian was destined to be a performer. From a young age, he demonstrated a natural gift for singing and a passion for music. His journey in the entertainment industry began at the tender age of 17 when he landed a role in the national touring company of the musical Wicked.

    Brian’s talent and dedication as a performer caught the attention of industry professionals, leading to several notable opportunities in the early years of his career. He starred in various musical theater productions, including Grease, Tarzan, and We Will Rock You. These performances allowed him to showcase his versatile vocal range and showmanship.

    Brian’s artistic prowess extends beyond musical theater, as evidenced by his participation in the ultimate rock show, QUEEN of the Night. Teaming up with the Gay Men’s Chorus of Los Angeles and also the rising star Alaysha Fox, Brian will channel the spirit of Freddie Mercury through a mesmerizing meld of talents.

    He Was a Finalist in AGT Season 11

    In 2016, Brian had a breakthrough moment that would change his life forever. He auditioned for the eleventh season of AGT and delivered a mind-blowing rendition of Queen’s “Somebody to Love.” His performance received a standing ovation from both the audience and the judges, leaving everyone in awe of his vocal prowess.

    Brian’s powerful voice, combined with his emotional delivery, resonated deeply with viewers across the nation. Week after week, he continued to impress the judges and captivate the audience, earning him a fouth place finish in the show’s grand finale. Despite not winning the competition, he is now famous as an exceptional vocalist in the music industry.

    Following his stint on AGT, Brian Justin Brian’s career skyrocketed. He released his debut single, “Show Me Love,” which garnered widespread acclaim and amassed thousands of views on YouTube. The success of his first single then led to opportunities to perform at prestigious events and venues worldwide.

    Brian’s unique ability to infuse raw emotion into his performances sets him apart as an artist. His soulful renditions of popular songs, such as Radiohead’s “Creep” and Phil Collins’ “In the Air Tonight,” showcase his incredible vocal range and ability to connect with his audience on a deeply emotional level.
