    COOKIES AND OTHER TECHNOLOGIES

    To help make MC Assets more responsive to the needs of users, including when you navigate and use MC Media as an MC Job Applicant, Morning Consult employs a standard feature of browser software called a “cookie.” Cookies are text files that assign each user a unique, random number and are stored in the internet browser or by the internet browser on a user’s computer system. Cookies do not personally identify the user; they merely identify the browser/computer with which the user accesses a given website. Unless users voluntarily identify themselves (e.g., through a subscription to information available via MC Media, or as an Authorized User, Authenticated User, or Subscriber), we will be unable to identify the identity of users through MC Assets, even if a cookie is assigned to users’ computers.

    The personal information a cookie can contain is information supplied by a user. Cookies prevent you from having to re-enter your preferences in certain areas of MC Assets where you have previously entered preference information. Most web browsers are initially set to accept cookies, but you can change your settings to notify you when a cookie is being set or updated, or to block cookies altogether. Consult the “Help” section of your browser for more information. Please note that by blocking any or all cookies, you may render your computer unable to take advantage of the personalized features enjoyed by other users of MC Assets.

    MC Assets’ servers automatically collect limited information about your computer’s connection to the Internet, including your IP address. The IP address is a number that lets computers attached to the Internet know where to send data – such as the web pages users view.

    Morning Consult also uses web beacons (also known as “single-pixel GIFs”) to access cookies and to count users who visit MC Assets or open Morning Consult’s HTML-formatted email messages, as well as to track device identification statistics and other non-identifying information for the purpose of analytics, push notifications, and error reporting. These analytics are provided to third party vendors.

    With respect to your application for employment with us, some of our online recruiting activities are hosted by third parties. When you access sites operated by these third parties, they may place cookies and other tracking technologies on your device.
