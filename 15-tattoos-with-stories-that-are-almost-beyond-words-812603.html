    1. “My tattoo guy said, ’That’s not a tattoo you copy, it’s a tattoo you earn.’”

    2. “After her son passed away, this amazing lady got a tattoo of the image and words from a card he had written for her.”

    “You’re here, you’re there, you’re everywhere...”

    3. “Sketch drawn by Grandpa tattooed on my arm.”

    4. “My wife’s simple but meaningful tattoo. Son was 10.5 inches at birth.”

    5. “It was to cover some scars that were left there that remind me of bad times.”

    7.“Matching cat tattoo, my best friend has the other half.”

    “It means ’the way of the warrior.’”

    11. “My dad got into a fatal car accident a little over 3 years ago and was a trucker since he was about 18 years old and absolutely loved eagles.”
